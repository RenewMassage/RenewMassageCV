About Sarah

Hi, I’m Sarah Johnson— I look forward to working with you. At Renew Massage Therapy, LLC my hope is for every client to feel renewed after a massage therapy session. Renewed in body, heart and mind. Renewed for their day, week and month.

I have been a licensed massage therapist since 2000. How time flies! I realize this may sound cliche; however, I have enjoyed every day that I’ve worked. Really. I learn something each day; I love that. I am a forever student. I’m also a massage educator at PHD Academy. I have plenty of real-life scenarios to share with students, who will be my future colleagues in this beloved massage therapy field.

Before starting Renew, I enjoyed being part of a local spa staff. Previous to that, I spent 11 years in the Twin Cities working in a wellness center as well as in conjunction with chiropractors. I am grateful for the learning and growing opportunities I experienced through those work environments.

At Renew Massage Therapy, each massage session is different depending on your specific needs, whether for relaxation (“swedish”) or firmer work (“deep tissue”). I customize with hot stones, cupping, and/or hot towels. I utilize a variety of techniques including, for example, myofascial release, MFLD, and muscle energy techniques to address your needs for that session.

Frequently Asked Questions

My first massage — what to expect?

Arrive about 15 minutes early so you’ll have time to fill out a little bit of paperwork and settle in. Then we’ll chat about what you’re hoping to get out of your massage—whether it's relaxation, pain relief, or just a moment to breathe. We’ll make sure you feel comfortable every step of the way. And remember your water bottle—hydration is key after a massage to help your body feel its best!

Do I need to bring anything to my appointment?

A filled water bottle—hydration is key after a massage to help your body feel its best! If you forget, it’s no big deal—just remember to hydrate when you get home.

I am not comfortable undressing — is that an issue?

Your comfort is the most important thing. Massage can be just as effective with clothing/undergarments on—we can adjust techniques to work through clothing or focus on areas like the hands, feet, neck, or shoulders. You’re always in control of what feels right for you.

I get nervous not knowing what to expect. Can you walk me through what an appointment is like?

Of course! After we chat about your goals for the session, I’ll step out so you can undress to your comfort level and get settled on the table. You’ll have plenty of time, and I’ll knock before re-entering to make sure you’re ready—if you need a few more minutes, that’s totally okay. We take professionalism seriously. You’ll always be properly draped with a sheet or blanket, and only the area being worked on will be uncovered. We’ll check in throughout the session to make sure you’re comfortable and respected the entire time.
